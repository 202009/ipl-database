My today’s recipe is potato wedges,which is not only delicious, yummyand spicy its simply amazing. Really very easy to make because all ingredients are used in this recipe are easily available at home. I present you, the mouth-watering dish, named as POTATO WEDGES.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes 3 to 4
  • Cooking oil 1 tablespoon
  • Red chilli powder ½ teaspoon but if you like more spicy so you can add more chilli powder.
  • Dried Parsley leaves
  • Salt as per taste
  • Black pepper as per taste

Recipe:

  • First clean the potatoes, wash them well then cut them in long strips shape. It should be thick
  • Boil 2 cups of water in a pan and add these raw potatoes in it. Boil them for 5 to 10 minutes. Make sure that these potatoes are not *fully* boiled.
  • In a bowl make seasoning. For that take 1 bowl add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil. Add above ingredients. Mix well.
  • Put the half boiled potatoes in this seasoning and mix well so that every wedge has been coated with a thin layer of oil.
  • Preheat the over for 10 minutes, on 200⁰C.
  • Grease the tray will a very thin layer of oil and place the raw wedges side by side in the tray.
  • Bake these for 20-25 minutes on 200⁰C.
  • Keep checking in between, take them out if a golden layer starts appearing over the potatoes.
  • Garnish it with some dried parsley and serve it with ketchup or ranch sauce.
  • Your heavenly potato wedges are ready to serve.
